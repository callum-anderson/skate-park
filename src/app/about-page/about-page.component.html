About Us

Tranent Skatepark Foundation was created with the intention of becoming a non-profit organisation for the community, to raise awareness towards community fundraising and to seek grant/funding applications for the project.

We aim to raise funds to secure a plot of land initially, to plan, design and then build a multi-purpose Skate/Bike Park within the town of Tranent.

Updates will be posted regularly to the updates section, so feel free to bookmark us!
